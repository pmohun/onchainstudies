Taste, Judgment, and Selection

I've been thinking about taste and selection in the age of AI. As a society, we are so obsessed with numbers. And for good reason — they reduce the unbearable complexity of our world into something that feels controllable. But judgment starts where numbers end, and that skill isn't going away even as intelligence becomes more abundant. These ten books are about developing and eventually learning to trust your intuition.

Phil Mohun, curator
