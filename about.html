High-signal reading clusters for people who build the future

I'm obsessed with books.

Always have been. I love to read them, I love to collect them, I love to browse through other people's collections.

This website is for people who are like me. Who know that the right book at the right time can change everything.

I started Onchain Studies because I was tired of reading alone. The best books deserve better than a highlight and a shelf. They deserve a conversation — with people who actually did the reading, noticed different things, and are willing to push back.

Each cluster is a group of books that don't look like they belong together until you start reading them.

If you've been looking for people who read like you do, we've been looking for you too.
